Our Story

Republique is an award-winning specialty coffee café in the heart of Stellenbosch, bringing the artistry of coffee and tea with a French flair to South Africa.

We are dedicated to sourcing only the finest single-origin beans and employing precision in every cup. Our atmosphere—clean lines, intentional detail, and refined elegance—whispers rather than shouts, inviting you to slow down and savor every moment.

With live music, free WiFi, and an array of light meals and French pastries, we've become the destination for serious coffee enthusiasts and locals alike.
